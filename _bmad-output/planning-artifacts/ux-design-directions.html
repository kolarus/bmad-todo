Evaluation Criteria

✓ Layout & Hierarchy

Which information hierarchy matches your priorities?

✓ Visual Density

Airy vs. compact — what feels right for your users?

✓ Interaction Style

Does it support your core "Type → Enter → See it" experience?

✓ Emotional Alignment

Does it evoke "calm competence" and restraint?

✓ Mobile Friendliness

Comfortable tap targets and thumb-reachable controls?

✓ Component Clarity

Are actions self-evident without explanation?

Each direction uses our established visual foundation (Slate/Indigo palette, system fonts, Tailwind) but creates a unique personality through layout, spacing, and component treatment.

Take your time exploring. You can choose one direction as-is, combine elements from multiple directions, or use one as a base and request modifications.
